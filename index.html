NSC 25

I participated in NSC 25 which is one of the most popular annual competition focusing on computer programming and software development skills in topic of general-using program. My team ideated the project named Scam Buster with the purpose to distinguish scammers in online world by using various datas about online scamming records.The project was successfully deployed in Android OS and we managed to get into final rounds of this competition. As a result of long-time development, I have learnt many things, More than just usual programming, about using API and have had first touch with Front-End works.
